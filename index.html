Simple. Elegant. Functional.

Bringing your personality to your desktop.

Mirth OS is a free, open-source, rolling-release operating system for people that love incorporating their personality into their desktop.

A big focus of Mirth OS is the ability to completely transform your desktop in a click. Give your desktop a unique look and feel that suits your workflow with both official and community made themes.

Why?

Why choose Mirth OS?

Well, here's why

Endless Freedom

Mirth OS doesn't limit you to anything. Choose from a selection of different themes to your liking, or if you're a DIY person, do anything it takes to transform your desktop the way you want it to be.

Hassle-free

Forget about updates. Mirth OS is a rolling release distribution based on Arch Linux. Install once and stay up-to-date, forever.

Minimal yet Elegant

Mirth OS uses lightweight window managers and minimal packages. All official installs consume less than 500MB of RAM at startup, and take up only 30GB of space at most

Safe and Secure

Mirth OS is completely open-source and does not collect any data from the user. Being open-source, anyone can inspect the code for vulnerabilities and quickly come up with a fix.
